## Dataset Description
 #### This dataset was obtained from Kaggle and contains 3,158 rows and 12 columns, representing agricultural, environmental, and economic factors related to farming activities in Karnataka, India. Each row corresponds to a specific agricultural record defined by location and year.
 #### The dataset combines climatic conditions, soil characteristics, irrigation details, crop information, and market-related variables, making it suitable for exploratory data analysis (EDA) in agricultural and climate-focused studies.

## The dataset has 12 column:

#### **Year**: The year when the agricultural data was recorded.
#### **Location**: The geographical region where the farming activity took place.
#### **Area**: The area of cultivation (likely hectares).
#### **Rainfall**: The total amount of rainfall (in millimeters) recorded for the location and year.
#### **Temperature**: The average temperature (in degrees Celsius) during the cultivation period.
#### **Soil Type**: The classification of soil in the farming area (e.g., Alluvial).
#### **Irrigation**: The method of irrigation used (e.g., Drip irrigation).
#### **Yields**: The total agricultural output (typically measured in kilograms or tons).
#### **Humidity**: The average atmospheric humidity percentage during the season.
#### **Crops**: The type of crop grown (in this case, mainly Coconut).
#### **Price**: The market price of the harvested crop.
#### **Season**: The agricultural season during which the crop was grown (e.g., Kharif or Rabi).
